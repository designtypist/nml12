Event Description

No Mans Lan is an event hosted at St.Clair College by The St Clair Information Technology Club (and eSports Gaming Events). The event was created to bring the community closer together by allowing people to interact with each other through video games. Players arrive and are able to gain access to the network that the ITC has created for the event. For sport or not, anyone is welcome and is able to enjoy the experience. With our connections branching out to Toronto from our friends at EGE we expect that our turn out will be larger then ever.
